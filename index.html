About

I am Saman!

Actively writing code and building high-functioning applications.

Software engineer, artist, and jack-of-all-trades who understands that deliberate, consistent progress over time is what counts most towards your progress and goals.

Having traveled extensively around the world - Thailand, the Phillippines, to Iran, to Brazil, and all over the US, Saman has a broad understanding of the world and a unique ability to connect with people from various backgrounds and cultures.

Through an exploration of roles and careers - from real estate investing, to marketing, to a manager at a coffee shop - Saman has gathered a plethora of skills that are highly applicable and versatile in work and business, equipping her with a unique hands on approach to solving complex challenges.

Saman strives to amplify the positive impact that each user can have by building personal solutions. In parallel, Saman believes that effective collaboration amplifies a team's impact. She is currently open to Software Engineering oppportunities on user-centric teams that drive impact collaboratively.
